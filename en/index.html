Welcome!

I am Valentin Joly*, a plant molecular biologist, bioinformatician, and PhD student from the University of Montreal, Canada.

My project at the Matton lab focuses on molecular mechanisms governing prezygotic breeding barriers in wild potato species. I am especially interested in pollen tube guidance, with a dual approach involving bioinformatics (next-gen sequencing) and molecular biology (protein expression and functional assays). Read more about my project in the Research page.
